Connect
Connect a thing to a country and gain points. You can choose the things from different categories:
  • car manufactures
  • dog breeds
  • tech companies
Remember: You get higher points, when you answer correctly and fast. To get top scores answer as many correct answer in row as you can and as fast as you can.
Trivia
Try to select a correct country by a set of trivia questions. Questions have different difficulty levels, so you get more points if you pick the correct country on more difficult questions.
