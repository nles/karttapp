Are you a geonerd?

Try these awesome games and learn something!
If you're smart enough, you might get yourself into the Hall-Of-Fame.

Connect
Connect a thing to a country and gain points. There are multiple categories you can choose from, so you learn some geography while pointing where your favorite things come from.
Flagger
Select a correct country by it's flag. With this game, you can memorize flags of different regions in a fun way!
